Executive Summary

This project introduces three novel academic metrics that overcome major flaws in the classic h-index: recency bias, collaboration inflation, and inconsistency. Empirical evaluation on 10 world-famous researchers shows our metrics provide fairer, more robust assessment.

Conclusion

The proposed metrics successfully address the known limitations of traditional indices. Freshness-Weighted h-index favors recent impact, CRI resists mega-collaboration inflation, and CLS rewards sustained excellence. This system represents a significant step toward fairer academic evaluation.
